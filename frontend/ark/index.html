Welcome to ArkWebMap!
ArkWebMap is the only website for viewing your Ark: Survival Evolved tames in your web browser. ArkWebMap supports the ability to...
  • View your Ark: Survival Evolved tribe tames in your web browser on a map
  • View stats and inventories of your tribe dinos
  • Check on baby dinos food and imprints and get feeding time estimates and alarms
  • Search tribe inventories
  • Use a wild dino heatmap, filterable to certain dinos
ArkWebMap is also a great tool for server owners. ArkWebMap is...
  • Open source
  • Easy for players to get going. Just sign into ArkWebMap with Steam and it'll work out servers and tribes they belong to
  • A small companion program you'll need to download to read your Ark server saves
  • Configurable. You can enable or disable certain features to your users
Not sure ArkWebMap is right for your server? Try the demo!
